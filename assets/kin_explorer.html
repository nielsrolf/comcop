Multiagent Dynamics Explorer

A lightweight, in-browser re-implementation of the two evolutionary experiments described in the doc. Configure the population and rules, then press Play — this runs entirely client-side, no server needed.

Dot color = agent's visible tag (kin-mode) or strategy (green = CooperateBot, red = DefectBot). Black squares = games. Reward matrix per round: mutual cooperate +1/+1, mutual defect 0/0, one-sided defect: defector +2, cooperator −1 — identical to games.py:get_reward for 2 players.
